Ashley Global Headquarters

1945 – Carlyle Weinberger founds Ashley Furniture Corporation as a sales organization with headquarters in Chicago, Illinois.

Ashley Furniture Industries is the #1 Furniture Manufacturer in the World. But our products aren't the only heroes.

The people of Ashley Furniture Industries also help this company truly define itself. Our team lives and breathes a “dirty fingernail” approach that breeds success unlike any other.

And it gets the job done. That's what we're made of and what it means to be Ashley made.

Ashley Furniture Industries in-house designers and engineers react quickly to consumer tastes, and have conquered the ability to make style and selection affordable.

Talented and ambitious, they travel the world to gain an understanding of the latest trends and discover emerging materials, constructions and technologies

Mastering the art of collaboration, they work closely with multiple departments to ensure products are produced efficiently.

This approach contributes to Ashley Furniture Industries ease of manufacturing, cost control, and speed to market.​
